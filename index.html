I am an assistant professor at the Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at University of Colorado Denver. My research interests include surrogate modeling with machine learning methods, uncertainty and sensitivity analyses, Monte Carlo methods, hydrological modeling, and subsurface energy storage.

Prior to coming to CU Denver, I worked as a postdoctoral fellow at Earth & Environmental Sciences Division at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, and a graduate assistant at the Department of Mathematics at the Florida State University.

Open Educational Resources (OER)

I am an advocate of Open Educational Resources (OER). I coauthored and published an OER book with Prof. Giray Ökten, and a second edition of the book in the format of Jupyter Book is being developed. There is also a collection of OER materials on Github provided by the Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at University of Colrado Denver.

  • Y. Liu and Giray Ökten, First Semester in Numerical Analysis with Python
  • A second edition of the book above (being developed) can be found at https://yaningliucudenver.github.io/Numerical-Analysis-I/bookintro.html. A set of Jupyter notebooks based on the book can be found at https://github.com/CU-Denver-MathStats-OER/Numerical-Analysis-I
